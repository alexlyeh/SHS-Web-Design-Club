About Us

The Saratoga High School Website Design Club is a club for passionate programmers and students who would like to expand their knowledge in web programming. Our club will use programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Our goal is to ensure that everyone leaves the school year with more familiarity in web programming, and even programming in general. Throughout the year, we try to find new businesses or companies that need websites, and create websites for them at a very cheap and reasonable price. All the money we earn from creating websites will be donated to charity.
